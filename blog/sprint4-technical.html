Tell your non-tech friend a story about a time you:

  • Got blocked on a simple problem

For sprint 4, I was supposed to run small coding tests. While doing the first challenge i.e. Variables and Objects I faced my first problem. It was uncomfortable in the beginning to know that I can’t solve the problem as it was supposed get difficult at later stage.

In the morning I asked one of my friends from the cohort and she told me that it is a test problem. After solving that problem, I was able to run test without error. The one thing that I learned from this was it is better to ask someone from the cohort who in the same boat.

For this problem I used ‘Asking your peers for help’ technique.
